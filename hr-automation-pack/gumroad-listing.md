### What's Inside

**5 HR Flow Templates (importable ZIP files + step-by-step setup guides):**

**Flow 1 — New Employee Onboarding**
New hire added to SharePoint → tasks created for IT, HR, and the manager → Teams welcome posted → welcome email to new hire. Zero manual steps.

**Flow 2 — Leave Request Approval**
Employee submits Microsoft Form → manager receives approval request → if approved: HR calendar updated + SharePoint leave register logged + employee confirmation email sent. If declined: employee notified automatically.

**Flow 3 — Monthly Timesheet Reminder**
Runs on the 25th of every month. Posts a branded reminder to your Teams channel and sends an all-staff email. Set it once, forget it forever.

**Flow 4 — Performance Review Reminder Sequence**
Daily check against your SharePoint list. Sends automatic email reminders at 30 days, 14 days, and 7 days before each scheduled review. 7-day reminder also alerts your HR Teams channel.

**Flow 5 — Employee Offboarding Checklist**
Employee departure logged in SharePoint → tasks auto-created for IT (access revocation, equipment), HR (exit interview, final pay), and the line manager (knowledge transfer) → Teams notification → manager email with action list.
