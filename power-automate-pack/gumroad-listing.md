## What's Inside

**5 Power Automate flow templates (.zip, importable directly into Power Automate):**

- **Flow 1 — New Employee Onboarding**
  Creates the Azure AD user, sends a welcome email, grants SharePoint access, and notifies the manager in Teams — all triggered from a single form submission.

- **Flow 2 — Manager Approval Workflow**
  Routes requests from a SharePoint list to the manager via a Teams adaptive card. Automatically updates the request status and notifies the requester whether it's approved or rejected.

- **Flow 3 — SharePoint Document Expiry Alert**
  Runs daily and emails document owners 30 days before their review date. Never let a compliance deadline slip through the cracks again.

- **Flow 4 — Teams Meeting Recap**
  Receives meeting notes via webhook, saves the recap to a SharePoint document library, posts a summary to your Teams channel, and emails all attendees.

- **Flow 5 — Automated Timesheet Reminder**
  Fires every Friday afternoon. Checks who hasn't submitted their timesheet for the week and sends them a direct Teams message — automatically.

**5 setup guides (PDF, 1–2 pages each):**

Each guide covers exactly what the flow does, what you need before you start, step-by-step configuration instructions, and the SharePoint list schema (where applicable). No guesswork.
