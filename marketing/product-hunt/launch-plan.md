# Product Hunt Launch Plan — Flowtech Advisory

## Launch Timing

**Best day:** Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday
**Post time:** 12:01 AM PST (6:01 PM AEST)
**Why:** PH resets the leaderboard at midnight PST. Posting at 12:01 AM gives a full 24 hours of voting. Avoid Monday (crowded with weekend builds) and Friday (lower traffic into weekend).

**Recommended launch date:** Pick a Tue-Thu with no major tech events or Apple keynotes competing for attention. Check producthunt.com/upcoming the week before to gauge competition.

## First Comment — Maker's Story

Post this immediately after launch (Product Hunt shows the maker's first comment prominently):

---

Hey Product Hunt! I'm Liam from Flowtech Advisory.

I spent years working in construction project management in Australia, and the amount of time teams waste on manual paperwork is staggering — expense claims getting lost, daily reports in random Word docs, budgets tracked across 15 different spreadsheets.

When I discovered Power Automate, I started building workflows to fix these problems on my own projects. Purchase order approvals that used to take 3 days happened in 3 minutes. Expense claims went from paper receipts in envelopes to AI-scanned, auto-routed approvals.

I kept getting asked by other PMs and site managers: "Can you share those templates?"

So I packaged everything up — 10 workflow templates, a beginner's course, Excel dashboards, and guides — all purpose-built for construction. No generic "business automation" fluff. Every template uses construction terminology, handles construction-specific edge cases, and works the way site teams actually operate.

The complete bundle is $99 (normally $151 if bought separately). Individual products start at $15.

I'd love your feedback. What other construction workflows would you want automated?
