## Meeting Rules

1. **Start on time.** No waiting for latecomers.
2. **No laptops** unless you're presenting or taking notes.
3. **Speak in outcomes**, not activities. "I had 6 meetings" is not a win.
4. **Blockers get 2 minutes.** If it needs more, take it offline with the right people.
5. **End with action items read aloud.** If an action item doesn't have an owner, it's not real.

---

## Tips for Founders

- **Rotate the facilitator** weekly so everyone practises running the meeting.
- **Archive these notes** in a shared folder (Notion, Google Drive, GitHub). They become invaluable context when onboarding new hires.
- **Review last week's action items first** — accountability compounds.
- **Kill the meeting** if there's nothing to discuss. A cancelled standup is better than a pointless one.
