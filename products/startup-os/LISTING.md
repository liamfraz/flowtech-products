## Description

**Run your startup like a company, not a group chat.** This 7-template operating system gives you the structure to set goals, track metrics, hire well, update investors, and make product decisions — without the overhead of enterprise tools you don't need yet.

Most early-stage founders waste months reinventing processes that already have proven templates. Weekly standups devolve into status theatre. OKRs get set in January and forgotten by February. Investor updates get skipped because "we'll send one when we have good news." This kit fixes all of that.

Every template is written in clean Markdown — paste into Notion, Google Docs, Linear, or wherever your team already works. No proprietary formats. No subscriptions. No 47-tab spreadsheets that take longer to learn than to use. Just practical, opinionated templates built for teams of 1–20.

**Built by operators, for operators.** These templates were refined across multiple startups, from pre-revenue side projects to Series A companies with real boards and real burn rates.

## What's Inside

- **Weekly Standup Template** — A 25-minute meeting framework covering wins, blockers, priorities, decisions, and action items. Includes facilitation rules that prevent status theatre and keep meetings productive.

- **OKR Tracking Template** — Quarterly Objectives and Key Results at company, team, and individual levels. Complete with scoring guides (0.0–1.0), a quarterly review summary, and tips for setting OKRs that are actually ambitious.

- **Hiring Pipeline Template** — Three documents in one: a job description framework, an interview scorecard with 7 evaluation criteria, and a complete offer letter template with equity details and acceptance signature block.

- **Investor Update Template** — Monthly update format with metrics dashboard, highlights, lowlights, product updates, financial summary, and specific asks. The format investors actually want to receive.

- **Product Roadmap Template** — Prioritised feature backlog with an impact/effort scoring matrix (1–5 scale), Now/Next/Later stakeholder view, shipped log, and a process for handling incoming requests without derailing your sprint.

- **Burn Rate Calculator Spec** — Complete specification for building a burn rate and runway calculator. Three sheets: monthly inputs with auto-calculated metrics, 12-month projection with conditional formatting, and a 3-scenario stress test planner.

- **Startup Metrics Dashboard** — The 7 SaaS metrics that matter (MRR, churn, CAC, LTV, NPS, ARPU, growth rate) explained in plain English with formulas, benchmarks, and a combined monthly tracking template.
