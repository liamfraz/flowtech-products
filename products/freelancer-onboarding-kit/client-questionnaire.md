## How to Use This Questionnaire

1. **Send** this document to your client via email or shared link before your first meeting.
2. **Review** their responses and flag any gaps or unclear answers.
3. **Discuss** during your kickoff call — use incomplete answers as conversation starters.
4. **Archive** the completed questionnaire in your project folder for reference throughout the engagement.
