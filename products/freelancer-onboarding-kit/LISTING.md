## Description

**Stop winging client onboarding.** This 6-template kit gives you a repeatable, professional intake process — from first contact to first invoice.

Whether you're a designer, developer, copywriter, or consultant, your onboarding sets the tone for the entire project. Sloppy intake = scope creep, missed expectations, and awkward money conversations. This kit eliminates all of that.

Every template is written in clean Markdown — easy to copy into Google Docs, Notion, your CMS, or any tool you already use. No proprietary formats. No subscriptions. Just practical templates you'll actually use on every project.

**Built by a freelancer, for freelancers.** These templates were refined over hundreds of client engagements across design, development, and consulting projects.

## What's Inside

- **Client Intake Questionnaire** — 25 structured questions covering scope, budget, timeline, brand, technical requirements, and communication preferences. Send before the kickoff call so you arrive prepared.

- **Proposal Template** — Professional proposal with executive summary, phased scope of work, deliverables table, timeline, pricing breakdown, payment terms, and T&Cs. Ready to customise and send.

- **Service Agreement (Contract)** — Simple, clear freelance contract covering scope, payment schedule, IP ownership, revision policy, confidentiality, and termination. Protects both parties without needing a lawyer.

- **Welcome Email Templates (×3)** — Project kickoff email, access & credentials request, and first meeting agenda. Send within the first 48 hours to set a professional tone.

- **Project Brief Template** — Structured document capturing goals, audience, scope, technical requirements, design direction, timeline, risks, and stakeholder contacts. The single source of truth for any project.

- **Invoice Template** — Clean invoice with line items, tax calculation, multiple payment methods, late fee clause, and tips for getting paid faster. Includes a numbering guide.
