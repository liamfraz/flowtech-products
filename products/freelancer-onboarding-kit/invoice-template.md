## Invoice Numbering Guide

Use a consistent numbering system across all clients:

| Format | Example | Best For |
|--------|---------|----------|
| `INV-YYYY-001` | INV-2025-001 | Sequential by year |
| `INV-[CLIENT]-001` | INV-ACME-001 | Per-client tracking |
| `INV-YYYYMM-001` | INV-202503-001 | Monthly billing cycles |

Pick one format and stick with it. Your accountant will thank you.

---

## Tips for Getting Paid Faster

1. **Send immediately.** Invoice the moment a milestone is delivered — don't wait until end of month.
2. **Make payment easy.** Include a direct payment link (Stripe, PayPal) so the client can pay in one click.
3. **Follow up on day 1.** If the due date passes, send a polite reminder the next day. Don't wait weeks.
4. **Use accounting software.** Tools like Xero, FreshBooks, or Wave automate invoicing, reminders, and tracking.
5. **Match the contract.** Your invoice milestones should mirror the payment schedule in your contract exactly.
