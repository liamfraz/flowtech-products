## 8. Termination

### By Either Party
This agreement may be terminated with **14 days' written notice** via email.

### Upon Termination
- The Client pays for all work completed up to the termination date
- The Freelancer delivers all completed work and work-in-progress
- The deposit is non-refundable
- Unused prepaid hours (if applicable) are refunded

### For Cause
Either party may terminate immediately if the other party:
- Breaches a material term of this agreement
- Fails to cure the breach within 7 days of written notice
