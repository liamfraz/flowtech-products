## What's Inside (5 Flows)

- **New Employee Onboarding** — Triggered when a new user is added to a SharePoint list. Automatically posts a welcome message to the team's Teams channel, sends the new hire a personalised welcome email with M365 access links, and adds them to the relevant SharePoint group. Eliminates the 12-tab manual onboarding checklist.

- **Document Approval Routing** — Routes documents added to a SharePoint library through a configurable approval chain. Approvers get an Adaptive Card in Teams with approve/reject/comment actions. Approval status updates back to the SharePoint item automatically. Works for policies, contracts, invoices — any document that needs sign-off.

- **SharePoint List Change Alerts** — Monitors one or more SharePoint lists for item creation or modification. Sends targeted Teams or email notifications to the right people (configurable by field value, not just a blanket blast). Replaces the built-in "Alert Me" with something that actually respects business logic.

- **Leave Request Approval** — Employees submit leave via a simple SharePoint form. The flow routes to their line manager via Teams Adaptive Card, handles approve/reject, writes the outcome back to the HR SharePoint list, and blocks out the calendar event automatically.

- **Weekly Team Digest** — Every Monday morning, pulls activity from a configurable SharePoint list (tasks, tickets, projects) and sends a structured summary to a Teams channel or distribution list. Includes items created, updated, and overdue in the past 7 days.
